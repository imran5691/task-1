A Paradise for Nature Lovers Cox’s Bazar Sea Beach, located on the shores of the Bay of Bengal in southern Bangladesh, is renowned for its beauty and is considered the largest sea beach in the world. Stretching for 120 kilometers, it attracts thousands of tourists from around the world each year. The beach is famous for its sunrise and sunset views and its diverse marine life, making it a must-visit for all nature enthusiasts.
